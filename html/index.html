Welcome

Welcome to PriceChart, an online service for inter-retailer price comparisons of consumer electronics. Currently [% num_products %] products from [% num_manufacturers %] manufacturers are being tracked across [% num_vendors %] retailers.

To start using the service, try searching for a product or brand in the search box at the top right.

PriceChart periodically looks up and saves the price information of different products. These prices are aggregated by product into charts. From these charts it can be easily determined which retailer currently has the lowest price for a given product.

Motivation

PriceChart is meant to be a consumer purchasing tool. It is intended to give the consumer insight into retail pricing which they would not normally have.

A common practice when purchasing something new is price comparison. A consumer decides on a certain product they are interested in and then look around for the best deal. That is what this service does. It automates price comparisons.

This is important because the price difference between different retailers for the same product is often hundreds of dollars if not more.
